TOKYO GHOUL CHARACTERS - JUUZOU SUZUYA

Cuteness Rating

10/10

About

Juuzou Suzuya is a special class investigator of the CCG (Commission of Counter Ghoul). His job is to eradicate every dangerous ghoul in sight to protect citizens from turning into dinner.

Appearance

Juuzou is a short, skinny, cute little investigator with shaggy white hair. He sports blue suspenders, a light pink button-down shirt with funky-colored buttons, red slippers, and baggy black trousers. He is never found without his red hairclips formed into the roman numeral XIII (13). Juuzou is also adorned in red stitches that can usually be found on his lip, eyelid, arms, neck, and sometimes earlobes.
