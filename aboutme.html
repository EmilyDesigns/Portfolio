About Me



Hello, my name is Emily A. Llauro and I am currently enrolled as a student at F.I.R.S.T. Institute.
In July of 2022, I will graduate with my diploma in Graphic Design and Web Development.
After graduation, I hope to combine my skills of illustration and graphic design to create
book covers, posters, and other various items.

When I was a child, I participated in traditional drawing classes which sparked my desire to
pursue art; however, I am almost entirely self-taught. I have always had a passion for creating
illustrations that I look forward to sharing with everyone.

In the future, I hope to use the experience I have gained to continue creating professional
illustrations and graphic design projects including manga covers and anime posters.
Since I have a great interest in anime, I want to contribute to promoting the growth of new
anime and manga with their merchandise and campaigns.
